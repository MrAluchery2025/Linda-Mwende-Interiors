Meet Lina Mwende

With over a decade of experience in interior design, Lina Mwende has transformed countless spaces into vibrant expressions of personality and function. Her passion lies in creating designs that blend elegance with everyday living, offering clients an experience that is both personal and professional.

From cozy residential homes to dynamic commercial spaces, Lina brings creativity, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of spatial harmony. Her work has been featured in several interior design publications, and she continues to innovate with each new project.

At Lina Mwende Interiors, every project begins with listening, followed by a collaborative process that ensures the final design not only meets but exceeds client expectations.
